  • ▸ Your software to manage your e-learnings

Creativity

The only limit of our platform is the imagination of the user. As a a learner or as a trainer, you can create innovative and meaningful contents.

Technologies

Because today, learning is not simply memorizing contents anymore, Claroline Connect wants to put all the potential of technology in the service of learning.

Collaboration

Everyone is responsible for his own training, but it is with the others that we learn best. Thanks to the collaborative tools of Claroline Connect, you can exploit all the power of the group.

Flexibility

Thanks to high technologies and a "Responsive Design "approach , Claroline Connect allows you to learn anytime, anywhere, on any device.
